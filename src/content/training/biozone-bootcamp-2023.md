A three-day intensive bootcamp designed for BioZone researchers, covering:

**Day 1: Machine Learning Basics**
- Introduction to supervised and unsupervised learning
- Hands-on practice with regression and classification
- Model evaluation and validation strategies

**Day 2: Application of ML to Bioscience**
- SVMs, decision trees, and ensemble methods
- Unsupervised learning for gene expression analysis
- Applying techniques to public bioscience datasets

**Day 3: Deep Learning**
- Neural networks and deep learning fundamentals
- Overview of GPT models and their applications
- Experimentation with pre-trained models
