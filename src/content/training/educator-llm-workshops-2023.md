CARTE has delivered multiple workshops to help educators navigate the opportunities and challenges of large language models in teaching and learning.

**Representative sessions:**
- *ChatGPT in the Classroom* – practical strategies for responsible use of LLMs in courses
- *LLMs Demystified* – technical overview for audiences at TMLS and other events
- Presentations at EdTech and research days on GPT, assessment, and academic integrity

Sessions combine live demonstrations with discussion of policy, ethics, and pedagogical design, helping instructors incorporate AI tools thoughtfully into their teaching.
