These tutorials for the Eric and Wendy Schmidt AI in Science Postdoctoral Fellows covered:

**Tutorial 1: Explainability and Fairness in ML (XAI)**
- Model interpretability (LIME, SHAP, feature importance)
- Visualizing model behavior and decision-making
- Fairness definitions, bias metrics, and mitigation strategies
- Real-world case studies

**Tutorial 2: Generative AI and NLP**
- Transformer architectures and self-attention
- Text generation, summarization, and dialogue systems
- Machine translation challenges
- Fine-tuning generative models for specific domains
