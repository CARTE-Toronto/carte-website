## CARTE is the hub for analytics and AI translation at the #1 engineering school in Canada.

![](https://carte.utoronto.ca/wp-content/uploads/sites/34/2020/11/carte-image.png)

As the hub for collaborations and partnerships in analytics and artificial intelligence at The University of Toronto's Faculty of Applied Science and Engineering, we conduct state-of-the-art research while nurturing the next generation of engineering talent. We seek to translate and commercialize effective solutions in engineering and be a catalyst for our partners to make a significant and lasting impact in the global marketplace.

### Vision

Engineering impact through Analytics and Artificial Intelligence (AI)

### Mission

*   To facilitate collaborative, cross-disciplinary basic and translational research in Analytics & AI
*   Attract, educate and develop expert personnel in Analytics & AI
*   Be a catalyst for partners to make a significant & lasting impact in the global marketplace

### Guiding Principles

*   Advancing analytics & AI research
*   Training analytics & AI experts
*   Making impact through analytics & AI
