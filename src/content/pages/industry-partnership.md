# Catalyze significant and lasting impact

## by leveraging advanced tools for insight generation and improved decision making

CARTE focuses on engineering applications and the translation of Analytics and Artificial Intelligence (A/AI) knowledge into real-world impact. It serve as a hub and matching platform, connecting U of T stakeholders with both external stakeholders, including industry, students and researchers.

The world is becoming increasingly data rich, there are critical needs to translate existing and emerging A/AI knowledge to create solutions with broad economic and societal impact. The are opportunities for all industries to exploit the pipeline of ideas, tools and talent developing in this space.
